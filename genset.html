Transforming technology into gentset monitoring

Harness the power of remote monitoring

An AI solution that helps to keep the power flowing

Syaa predictive maintenance platform ensures the lights stay on when you need them most.

Syaa AI-powered platform is designed to help you detect issues with your genset long before you realize there’s a problem.

Syaa proprietary vehicle health monitoring system transforms genset maintenance into predictive maintenance.

We leverage Digital Twin Technology to create a virtual copy of a genset, then analyze historical and real-time data to provide early warnings of equipment issues. The result: a significant decrease in breakdowns, which means lower maintenance costs and enhanced engine uptime.

By identifying maintenance issues before they occur, Intangles can help you save time and money.

Generator performance:
  • Real-time genset running status monitoring
  • Get online access to generator regardless of its location
  • Monitor parameters like engine temperature, oil pressure, battery voltage, power output, engine run-time, engine RPM, diesel levels
  • Take timely actions to improve performance
    • Reduced maintenance costs:
    • Get real-time information like battery voltage, temperature trends and hours run
    • Remote fault diagnosis
    • Schedule maintenance more easily
    • Reduced sudden downtime
    • Cut-down maintenance cost and fuel consumption
