Boost your fleet’s ROI

Boost your fleet’s ROIwith predictive analytics

Predict truck breakdowns days before they happen, optimize your fleet’s performance and reduce fuel costs with AI-powered platform.

Telematics is designed to keep trucks on the road – not in the shop.

Telematics proprietary vehicle health monitoring system transforms fleet maintenance into predictive maintenance.

We leverage Digital Twin Technology to create a virtual copy of a truck, then analyze historical and real-time data to provide early warnings of vehicle issues. The result a significant decrease in breakdowns, which means lower maintenance costs and enhanced uptime.

By identifying maintenance issues before they occur, can help you save time and money.

A global solution for the entire trucking industry

A global solution for the entire trucking industry Telematics platform works seamlessly with major makes, models, and engine types all over the world.

  • Diesel, gasoline, propane, CNG, LNG and EVs – our solution is designed to perform in them all.
  • We support over 2,000 engine configurations and are adding more every day.
  • Our hardware can be easily installed seamlessly alongside any existing telematics device.
